The virtual experience


Take your gaming to a whole new level. Explore the gaming worlds with full controls over your characters movement. The headgear also has engine integration so you can develop your own worlds.

Experience the freedom you've always wanted in massive singleplayer worlds, explore and discover the massive sprawling world of virtual MMO's with other people .


Reverie will come with the publicly available SDK which includes source code, documentation, and samples to help you hit the ground running. The SDK currently support Windows, Mac OS X, and Linux.

Tech Specs

Below you will find the technical specs of the machine. Not all hardware are listed below.

  • HDMI output (HDMI Included)
  • USB 2.0 & 3.0 ports
  • 4x Batteries(2600mAh)

  • 2GB of memory
